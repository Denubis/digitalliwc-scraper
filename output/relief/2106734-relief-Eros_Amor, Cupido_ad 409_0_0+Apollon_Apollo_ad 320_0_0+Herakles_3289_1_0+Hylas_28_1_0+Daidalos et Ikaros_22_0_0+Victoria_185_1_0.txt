	Beschreibung:	Daidalos, bearded and wearing tunic, seated, working on wing which is held erect on a tall block between his knees. In another scene Hylas, with chlamys, two spears and jug, moving left to draw water. On the right a crouching nymph reaching out to grab Hylas. On the left are two nymphs. Victoria seated, holding the flapper of a dolphin.
