	ID:	2625
	Objekt:	sarcophagus lid
	Material:	marble
	Land:	CH
	Kategorie:	relief
	Name:	Delos I Leto Apollon/Apollo Hekatoncheires Kynthos Zeus/Iuppiter Athena/Minerva Iuno Iris I Artemis/Diana Demeter/Ceres Eileithyia Hekatoncheires Leto/Latona
	Beschreibung:	To the extreme left between two figures interpreted as Kynthos and Leto, a bearded person (Aegaeon)) carrying a young woman (Delos). Leto prepares to give birth on Delos. To the right Iris anounces the proximity of the delivery to two sitting goddesses. The very restored figure between the goddesses could be Eileithyia who would be hurrying to aid Leto. Alternative interpretation: birth of Apollo and Diana, around them Ceres, Proserpina and Eileithyia assisting; or: the young Apollo is presented to the Capitolina Triad and the other gods: in the centre Iuppiter seated, Minerva by his right side, Iuno bis his left.
