	Beschreibung:	Young naked Bacchus with ivy crown; long hair; thyrsos staff; waiting till Ariane sleeps. At the left middle a temple at the base of which sits a woman. Striding away from her - toward the shore - a man. On the shore a woman reclines near an altar, another leans against a column. At the middle right a bronze statue of seated Poseidon on a rocky ledge. In the middle a ship sailing on the sea. Above it, small Daidalos flies left. The badly deteriorated upper section once included Helios in the quadriga and the falling Ikaros.
