	Beschreibung:	A succession of paintings in a frieze: (i) Herakles kills the ketos; (II) Telamon approaches the seated Laomedon (I), in oriental dress to demand the horses; (iii) Herakles, nude with raised club, kills Laomedon (I), i oriental dress and with sceptre, in the presence of his frightened courtiers; (iv) the wedding of Telamon and Hesione; (v) Herakles crowns the young Priamos. Priamos asking Achilleus for the corpse of Hektor. Idaios in oriental dress with a vessel on his shoulder. In another scene Priamos and Idaios sitting outside in front of a steep rock. In another scene Herakles on the pyre.

	Beschreibung:	An old priest with a shaven head is clothed in a white gown, holding a patera and a situla in his right hand. In his left hand one can see a sistrum. Due to the attribution of the situla and the sistrum to the goddess Isis one could speculate if this priest is in fact a priest for the goddess Isis herself. Partly erased inscription beneath his feet. The Hora of Summer with sickle is depicted too - she reminds of some representations of Aphrodite.
