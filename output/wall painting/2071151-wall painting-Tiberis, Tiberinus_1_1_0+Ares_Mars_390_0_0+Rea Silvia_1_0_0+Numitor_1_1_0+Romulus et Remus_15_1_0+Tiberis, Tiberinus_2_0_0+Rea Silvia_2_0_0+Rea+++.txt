	Name:	Aineias Ares/Mars Askanios Latinus Lavinia Lavinium Mezentius Numicius, Numicus Numitor Rea Silvia Romulus Tiberis, Tiberinus Turnus Victoria Remus
	Beschreibung:	Eastern wall: Latinus, Aineias and Lavinia. Other interpretation: Amulius, Antho, Numitor and Silvia. Then Lavinia standing between two seated women, Amata and her sister. In the background further women. Other interpretation: Rea Silvia, her mother and her companions. Southern wall: An aged woman (Amata?) and Turnus, Lavinia (inscribed) enthroned. To the left on the middle of the south wall Mezentius wearing red exomis, blue baldric and holding squared shield shakes hands with Askanios. Close to the walls of Lavinium, on the right of a battle scene, a bearded person seated on the ground, wearing crown of leaves, must be Numicius.
