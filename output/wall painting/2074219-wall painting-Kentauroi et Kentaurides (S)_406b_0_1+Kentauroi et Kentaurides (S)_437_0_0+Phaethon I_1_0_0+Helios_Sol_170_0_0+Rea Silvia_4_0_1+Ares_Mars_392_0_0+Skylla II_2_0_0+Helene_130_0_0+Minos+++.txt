	Beschreibung:	Fresco of the Domus Aurea in Rome, today lost; drawing preserved; two women holding liknon with phallos (?) over small covered person. Amaltheia carries infants (Zeus and Hera?) on her back. Three women around a dying Semele. Two are washing the baby Dionysos, the third standing before Semele and holding her hand, probably Eileithyia (youth of Bacchus). Girls on the shore are watching Europe (I), half naked, mantle in arc over head, reclining on back of the bull galloping over the sea to the right. Hercules kissing the hand of Minerva. Phaethon (I) in front of Helios in the presence of the Horae. Zeus holds the baby Herakles to the breast of the sleeping Hera in the presence of Athena and Hypnos. Dionysos/Bacchus with thyrsos, around him two dancing maenads (or nymphs). Skylla brings the lock of hair to Minos (I). Mars approaching the sleeping Rea Silvia.
