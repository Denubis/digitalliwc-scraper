	Beschreibung:	The upper left ist largely destroyed. At the top centre Helios in his quadriga. At the top right, below him, Ikaros with winged arms outstretched falling backward. Slightly lower and to the left the winged Daidalos strides toward a distyle temple or tomb, against which rests a painted tablet. Opposite, on a rocky ledge, a bronze seated Poseidon. In the bay at the centre a ship and on the sea below a boat with two oarsmen with raised arms. In the centre foreground on the shore a draped woman leans on a column, staring upward.
