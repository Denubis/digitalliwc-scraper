	Objekt:	wall painting
	Herkunft:	Roman
	Land:	CH
	Kategorie:	wall painting
	Stichwort:	altar fire (on altar) temple sanctuary woman warrior, soldier
	Name:	Athena Athena/Minerva Hero Kadmos I Orpheus Phyllis Akamas and Demophon Demophon Leander
	Beschreibung:	In front of a sanctuary a warrior and a woman hugging each other. On the left another warrior, on the right a girl leaving. Above the afired altar a woman pending. Above the scene Athena. The identification of the figures is contested (Iason and Medeia, Protesilaos and Laodameia, Demophon and Phyllis, Kadmos and Harmonia). In another scene Leander swimming to the left. Hero waits on the other side of the river in a tower. On the right shore a fisherman.
	Bibliographie:	Reinach, RépPeint 22, 5; HBr 209-210 fig. 61 pl. 152; Dawson, Ch.M., YaleClSt 9, 1944 101 n. 43 pl. 15; Schefold, WP 133;Thompson, M.L., in Essays in Memory of K. Lehmann (1964) 329-343 fig. 1.
