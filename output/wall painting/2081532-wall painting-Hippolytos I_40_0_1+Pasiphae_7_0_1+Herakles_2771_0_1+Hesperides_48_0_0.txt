	Name:	Herakles Hesperides Pasiphae Hippolytos I
	Beschreibung:	Cubiculum q: (Hesperiden:) Die Hesperiden rund um einen von Schlange umwundenen Rundaltar. Vor ihnen, am Boden, ein runder Korb (?) mit Amphora und anderen Dingen? Lower right and part of left side of painting not preserved. Daidalos seated on framework platform or scaffolding and holding in right hand a small model of a mechanical cow which he shows to Pasiphae, right hand raised toward her face looking toward Daidalos and the model. Between Daidalos and Pasiphae stands an Eros.
	Bibliographie:	Schefold, WP 72;Dawson, C.M., Romano-Campanian Mythological Landscape Painting (YaleClSt 9, 1944) 89 Nr. 22 Taf. 7;PompPittMos III (1991) 843-844, 23-24.
