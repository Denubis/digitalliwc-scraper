	Name:	Agamemnon Aias I Gorgo, Gorgones Nestor
	Beschreibung:	Nestor sitting to the right, with spear and shield. Behind him stands Thrasymedes. In front of Nestor are Odysseus, Agamemnon and Diomedes. At the right Achilleus is sitting on his shield and behind him Patroklos. In another scene Odysseus seated with spear and sword. To the left on the ground are warrior's arms, Nestor and Achilleus. To the right Diomedes and Pallas Athena. In another scene Achilleus seated on a rock with spear and sword, looking towards Nestor who seems to talk to him. Behind him Thrasymedes shield on the ground. In the middle Patroklos leading a horse. From the same Domus fragments of gorgoneia in opus sectile.
