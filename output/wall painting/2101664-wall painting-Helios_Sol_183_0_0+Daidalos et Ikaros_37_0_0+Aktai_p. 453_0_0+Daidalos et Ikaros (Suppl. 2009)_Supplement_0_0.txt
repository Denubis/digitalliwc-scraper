	Beschreibung:	Steep crags frame the fall of Ikaros. At top centre Helios in his quadriga. Below him, nude Ikaros falling backward with wings still attached and arms outstretched. To his right Daidalos, winged and wearing cloak, flies to his left toward a tree on crag. Below Daidalos a boat with three men rowing. At the left a statue of Poseidon, seated. At the lower right a seated fisherman. On the shore between them, a group of standing women.
