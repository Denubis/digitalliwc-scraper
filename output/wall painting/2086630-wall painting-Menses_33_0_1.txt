	Name:	Menses
	Beschreibung:	Fragmentary fresco calendar from a porticoed building under the Church of Santa Maria Maggiore in Rome. Painted frieze in which each month had not only a panel of fasti and brief notations of appropriate agricultural operations, but was also illustrated by zodiacal signs and a tableau of rural scenes. The scenes of two Menses are sufficiently preserved to identify them partially: September picking apples and November gathering olives and ploughing fields.
