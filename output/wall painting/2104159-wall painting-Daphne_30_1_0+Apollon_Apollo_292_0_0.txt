	Beschreibung:	Wall painting from the Casa die Meleagro. Daphne stands on the left, half-draped, leaning on a pillar, lifting one end of her cloak over her left shoulder and gazing at Apollon, who is nearly naked, wreathed and with sandals, playing the kithara and looking back at her. A quiver is set against another pillar behind him.
