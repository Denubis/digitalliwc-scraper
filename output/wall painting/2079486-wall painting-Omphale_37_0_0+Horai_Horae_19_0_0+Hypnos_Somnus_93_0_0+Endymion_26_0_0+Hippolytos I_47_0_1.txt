	Beschreibung:	Herakles seated on his lionskin on a rock, with club, dressed in headcloth, woman's dress and shoes, holding spindle and thread. At either side women - at the right one standing, one seated, both half-nude, and a satyr holding thyrsos and tympanon. On the left a seated dressen woman (Omphale?) who points at Herakles, a kalathos beside her.  In another scene three Horae: Summer with sheaf of corn and sickle. Autumn with basket and a bunch of grapes. Winter with a stick and two ducks.
