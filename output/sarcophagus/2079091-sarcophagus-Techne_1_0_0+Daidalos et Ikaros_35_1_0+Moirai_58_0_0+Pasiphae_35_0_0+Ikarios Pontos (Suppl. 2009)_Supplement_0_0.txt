	Name:	Moirai Pasiphae Techne Daidalos and Ikaros Ikaros Ikarios Pontos
	Beschreibung:	Representation of Ikaros' myth. Fabrication of the wings; their fixing; fligh; death; To the right side over the dead body of Ikaros, representation of a recumbent bearded male figure on a rock holding a branch, his legs covered by a mantel (figure intepreted as Ikarios Pontos). Scene 1 shows Daidalos fashioning a wing. Behind him a diademed female reaches out to assist him (Techne). She has been identified as Pasiphae, Athena or Ars.
	Bibliographie:	Calderone, S., in: Romanitas christianitas. Untersuchungen zur Geschichte und Literatur der römischen Kaiserzeit. J. Straub zum 70.
