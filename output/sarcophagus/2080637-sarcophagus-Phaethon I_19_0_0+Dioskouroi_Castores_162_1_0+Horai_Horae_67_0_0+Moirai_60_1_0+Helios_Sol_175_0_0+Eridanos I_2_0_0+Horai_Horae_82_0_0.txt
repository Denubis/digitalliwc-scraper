	Name:	Eridanos I Phaethon I Dioskouroi/Castores Horai/Horae Moirai Helios/Sol
	Beschreibung:	On the extreme left Phaethon (I) begging Helios for his chariot in the presence of the four Horai, the Winds and Aurora. Then the fall of Phaethon (I) is depicted: Eridanos (I) to the right of Phaethon (I) who is hitting his head on the ground in the presence of a paidagogus, Kyknos and the Dioskouroi or Phosphoros and Hesperos. On the right Hermes, three Heliades, Klymene and a genius (or: the mourning Sol receiving the sad news from Mercurius).
