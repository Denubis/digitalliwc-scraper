	Name:	Charon I Hektor Ixion Protesilaos Sisyphos I Tantalos Eidola Nekyia Mercurius
	Beschreibung:	Scenes from the life of Protesilaos: Next to the passing away Protesilaos stands his Eidolon. Then Mercurius, guiding the Eidolon to Charon. Charon wearing the exomis, is standing in his boat part of which disappears under the gate of Hades. He holds the pole and reaches out to Protesilaos who is being handed over to him by Hermes. On the right minor face Sisyphos, Ixion fixed to the wheel and Tantalos.
