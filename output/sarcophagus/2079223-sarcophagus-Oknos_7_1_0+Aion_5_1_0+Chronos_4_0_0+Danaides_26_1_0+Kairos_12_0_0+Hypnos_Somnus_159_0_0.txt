	Name:	Aion Chronos Danaides Fata, Fatum Hypnos/Somnus Kairos Kerberos Nemesis Oknos Thanatos
	Beschreibung:	On the front, in seven openings of the arcade, from left to right the Danaides, Oknos and the ass, Kerberos, a nude figure that rests its left foot on a wheel, a nude aged and winged person with scales and a torch, and a skeletal larua leaning on an inverted torch. Le Glay suggests that the figure with the scales may be Chronos, but it could be Hypnos, Thanatos or Kairos as well.
