	Beschreibung:	On the front: A scene representing the Indian triumph of Bacchus, the god in his triumphal chariot drawn by two panthers, ridden by two black boys, watched by the protective eye of a young satyr; On the lid, on the left: Birth of Bacchus from Iuppiter, assisting him Mercurius and Eileithyia; In the centre: Mercurius carrying Bacchus away (to the nymphs at Nysa); On the right: Nude youth (Daphnis) with lagobolon and skin sending an old papposilen to the child Dionysos and the nymphs. To the right of Daphnis below a tree Pan sitting.
