	Name:	Eros/Amor, Cupido Minos I Pasiphae
	Beschreibung:	The long side of the sarkophagus is divided into three vertical sections, each overlapping the next. The ends of the sarcophagus are framed by Erotes, one on either side. From left to right: 1) Pasiphae commissionong Daidalos. Pasiphae enthroned, Eros beside her, Daidalos in relaxed pose. 2) Daidalos and artisans building the mechanical cow. 3) Daidalos presenting the mechanical cow to Paisphae. The short side panels bear Minos standing in front of a temple or shrine of Poseidon. The old woman has been identified as Minos' mother Europa. On the other minor side three youths identified as Katreus, Deukalion and Androgeos (sons of Minos and Pasiphae).
