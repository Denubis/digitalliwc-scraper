	Name:	Aigisthos Elektra I Klytaimestra Orestes
	Beschreibung:	On the left Orestes and Elektra at the grave of Agamemnon (only Orestes preserved). In the centre, Orestes delivers the false news to Aigisthos. Aigisthos is seated on a throne, surrounded by his men. On the right, Orestes in the background attacking Aigisthos who is seated on a throne, his mantle about his lower body. Klytaimnestra sits next to him. In the foreground, the old nurse and Elektra or a Fury. At the extreme left Pylades. All scenes are separated by pillars.
