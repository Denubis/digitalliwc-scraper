	Name:	Alkestis Athena/Minerva Deukalion I Helios/Sol Kaukasos Kyklopes Hermes/Mercurius Moirai Prometheus Psyche Tellus Vulcanus Luna Mercurius
	Beschreibung:	The front side shows a depiction of the creation of mankind by Prometheus; aiding him is Minerva; Sol and Luna on chariots framing the scene. Mercurius approaching with Anima. On the minor sides are Hephaistos and three Cyclopes and under a tree the first human couple: either Deukalion (I) and Pyrrha or Atlas and a Hesperid.
