	Name:	Aigisthos Elektra I Erigone I Klytaimestra Oiax Orestes Pylades Oiax
	Beschreibung:	Death of Aigisthos: Orestes, nude except for a cloak, in "hair-grabber" pose, attacks Aigisthos who is already wounded and seated on a throne. Aigisthos bearded, wearing mantle about his lower torso, trying to push Orestes away. To the right of Aigisthos a female figure raising a footstool over her head (Klytaimnestra? or Erigone (I)?). On the left of Orestes Elektra approaching. Behind her, Pylades and a woman fleeing. At the extreme left a Fury holding a torch. Right half: Death of Klytaimnestra. Orestes attacks her, Oiax tries to interfere.
	Bibliographie:	SarkRel II pl. 54 no. 154; Brommer, Denkmälerlisten III 322, 1; Vermeule, E., AJA 70, 1966, 18 no. 32; Sichtermann, H., Ein ungewöhnlicher mythologischer Sarkophag, RM 78, 1971, 189 pl. 94, 1 .
