	Name:	Aietes Hippotes Iason Kreousa II Medeia Merope II Sphinx (Thebana) Tellus
	Grössenangabe:	L: 217 cm; H vorne: 65 cm; H hinten: 55 cm
	Beschreibung:	Medea sarcophagus: The wreathed young men is maybe Hippotes, the brother of Kreousa (II). The children of Medeia handing over presents to Kreousa (II). Minor sides: Crouching female Sphinx.
