	Name:	Achilleus Automedon Briseis Hektor Idaios Hermes/Mercurius Thetis Mercurius
	Beschreibung:	Three scenes: On the right kneeling Priamos asking Achilleus for the corpse of Hektor. Mercurius and Briseis in the background. In the centre Achilleus on his chariot dragging Hektor. On the left the chariot of Priamos from which Trojans are unloading goods. Left minor side: Veiled Thetis, Achilleus and servants.
	Bibliographie:	Vokotopoulou; ArchDelt 30 B 2 (1975) 211-213 pl. 119-120, 2; Linant de Bellefonds, P., Sarcophages attiques de la nécropole de Tyr(1985)37 no. 3;  Giuliani, L., JbBerlMus 31 (1989) 28-29  fig. 3.
