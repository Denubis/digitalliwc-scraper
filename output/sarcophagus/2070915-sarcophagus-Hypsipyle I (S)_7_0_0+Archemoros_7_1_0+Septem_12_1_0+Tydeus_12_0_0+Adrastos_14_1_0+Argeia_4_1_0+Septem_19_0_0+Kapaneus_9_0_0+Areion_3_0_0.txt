	Land:	CH
	Kategorie:	relief
	Stichwort:	sword shield spear horse
	Name:	Adrastos Archemoros Areion Argeia Hypsipyle I Kapaneus Tydeus Septem
	Beschreibung:	Seven leaders, one of them Adrastos wearing short chiton and chlamys with his horse Areion. On the right minor side Archemoros kneeling on the ground, entangled by the snake. He looks at his nurse Hypsipyle. From the left a hero approaches to fight against the snake. On the left edge Argeia and her sister Deïpyle handing over a helmet to Polyneikes. The person with the skin of a boar is interpreted as Tydeus.
	Bibliographie:	Johnson, F. P., Corinth IX, Sculpture 1896-1923 (1931) 114-119 no. 241 with fig.; Daumas, M., AntK 21, 1978, 24-31 pl. 7, 7.
